1- COMING SOON – MARCH 2023 – Do you want to travel to Spain?

Currently writing a book on how to plan a successful trip to Spain – focusing on Andalusia and Costa del Sol. This book is a friendly guide customizing your vacation. From creating an exciting itinerary to reserving seats on the Ave train (to minimize travel time) to booking a locally sourced tour - this is your blueprint. Why did I choose to write this book? For many weeks, I tried to hire a travel agent - who had firsthand experience in Spain post-COVID. Not one candidate was a good match for my travel plans, so I decided to plan it by myself. It took months. So, I decided to write a quick and easy book for all future vacationers. This little gem will save readers weeks of frustration and countless hours of tedious research.



2- COMING SOON – MARCH 2023 – Writing Your College Admission Essay is as easy as cooking GUMBO!

Final edit stage. This book is a fun way to address an extremely serious topic – the college admission essay. Most high school seniors feel the enormous pressure of producing a ‘publish-worthy’ essay. Sometimes the college-bound student needs a lighter lens to view the task at hand. Because this book is written in a recipe format, it is both entertaining and easy-to-follow. Why did I write this book? It was August 2019. A stressed parent hired me as a college consultant to help their struggling senior. She was having trouble writing even an early rough draft of her application essay. So, just for her I typed out a manuscript using my favorite Louisiana Gumbo recipe as a format. Even though the student had never tasted gumbo (much less cooked the dish in real life), it was the key that unlocked the writer’s block.
